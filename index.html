About Us

Our Story

Founded a little bit different from other businesses.

Our story goes way back to 2020 during the covid lockdown. Where inspired by a lot of people who ventured into the small business world during the trying times. We decided to put our foot into the waters too.

Piqued with the interest of resin art for the longest time there was no other business for us to venture in.

We began planning and crafting and then sooner rather than later Silicity was born in August 2021.
